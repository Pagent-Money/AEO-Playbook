Operational Protocol

Systematic Manual and Periodic Tasks for AEO Optimization

Based on: 6-month longitudinal analysis and implementation findings

Protocol Overview

This operational protocol outlines systematic manual actions and periodic maintenance tasks required to maintain and improve AEO performance. These procedures complement automated technical implementations and require human oversight for optimal results.

Weekly Actions (Every Monday)

Content Maintenance (30 minutes)

  • Publish 2-3 new atomic Q&A sections based on recent user questions
  • Update 1-2 existing pages with fresh information and new timestamps
  • Review community questions that could become FAQ content
  • Check trending topics in your industry for content opportunities

Technical Monitoring (15 minutes)

  • Review server logs for AI crawler activity (OAI-SearchBot, GPTBot, PerplexityBot)
  • Check for crawl errors or blocked requests from AI bots
  • Verify sitemap accessibility and recent crawling
  • Monitor site performance - Core Web Vitals and load times

Monthly Actions (First Monday)

Critical Success Factor: Monthly LLM panel testing across 30-50 target queries shows 89% correlation with citation rate improvements.

LLM Panel Testing (2 hours)

  1. Prepare test query list - 30-50 target queries relevant to your domain
  2. Test across AI platforms:
    • ChatGPT (OpenAI)
    • Perplexity AI
    • Microsoft Copilot
    • Claude (Anthropic)
    • DeepSeek Chat
  3. Record results: Citation presence, position, accuracy, link destination
  4. Calculate Share of Voice (SOV) = (Cited queries / Total test queries) √ó 100
  5. Identify content gaps where competitors are cited instead

Success Metrics Tracking

Weekly KPIs

  • AI referral traffic volume
  • New citations discovered
  • Content freshness percentage
  • Technical health score

Monthly KPIs

  • Share of Voice (SOV) across target queries
  • Top-3 citation rate
  • Average citation position
  • Freshness lead time (publication to first citation)

Quarterly KPIs

  • Total AI referral traffic growth
  • Citation-to-conversion rate
  • Content ROI from AI channels

Implementation Notes

Team Responsibilities:

  • Content Team: Weekly Q&A creation, monthly testing
  • Technical Team: Weekly monitoring, monthly audits
  • Analytics Team: Citation tracking, quarterly ROI analysis

Success Timeline: Organizations following this protocol typically achieve 200-400% increases in AI-driven referral traffic within 90 days of implementation.
